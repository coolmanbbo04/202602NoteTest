# Privacy Policy（Template）— humanvip.com

Last updated: 2026-02-19

## 1. Overview
This web app provides note-taking features including optional share links and optional voice dictation (browser capability).

## 2. Data We Store
- Notes content you create (title, content).
- Share identifier if you generate a share link.

## 3. Data We Do NOT Intentionally Collect
- We do not require account registration.
- We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal identifiers.

## 4. Voice Input
Voice dictation is processed by your browser/OS speech recognition capability. Microphone permission is requested by the browser. We do not receive raw microphone audio unless your browser/provider sends it as part of speech recognition (varies by browser).

## 5. Cookies / Local Storage
We use localStorage to store:
- pinned notes preferences
- offline drafts for reliability

## 6. Data Sharing
If you generate a share link, anyone with the link may view the shared note.

## 7. Data Retention
Notes persist until deleted by the user (or per deployment policy).
